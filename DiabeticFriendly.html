Diabetic Friendly Application

Completely Diabetic friendly recipe

    Calculator

Diabetic Friendly Recipe is an application to view recipes that are beneficial to people suffering from diabetic

This application can list out different styles of dishes as well as the calories associated with it. They also have option to view the benefits of the food.

Diabetic type 2 is a disease mostly controllable by food and exercises. Many people strive hard to understand the food that helps to control diabetics. This mobile application helps the user to find the recipe that match their taste and make them control their sugar level.
